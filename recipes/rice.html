White Rice

Perfect rice, brazilian recipe

Ingredients
  • 1 cup of rice
  • 2 cups of boiling hot water
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1/4 slicedonion
  • salt to taste
  • olive oil to fry teh onion and garlic
Steps Guide
  1. saute the garlic and onion
  2. put the rice and let it fry for 30 seconds
  3. put the boiling hot water
  4. let it cook for 20 minutes, or until the water almost dry out
And thats it, it's done, enjoy!
