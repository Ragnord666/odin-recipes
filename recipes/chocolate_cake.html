Chocolate Cake

Flufy and tasty chocolate cake, easy recipe to do

Ingredients
  • 2 cups of wheat flour
  • 1 cup of surgar
  • 1 cup of chocolate powder
  • 1/2 cup(tea) of soy oil
  • 1 cup of warm water
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder
Steps Guide
  1. Grease your cake pan
  2. Preheat your oven at 140 degrees
  3. mix all Ingredients in the blender for 4 minutes, except the baking powder
  4. mix the baking powder for 30 seconds
  5. pour everything in the cake pan, and bake it for 40 minutes with the oven at 140 degrees
And thats it, it's done, enjoy!
