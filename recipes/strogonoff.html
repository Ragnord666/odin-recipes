Brazilian Chicken Stroganoff

Brazilian Chicken Stroganoff is a delicious classic!

Ingredients
  • 3 Chicken breast sliced in small cubes
  • 1 minced garlic clove
  • salt and peper to taste
  • 1 sliced onion
  • 1 spoon of mayo
  • 1 spoon of butter
  • 1/2 cup of ketchup
  • 1/3 cup of mustard
  • 1 cup of milk cream
  • potato sticks to taste
Steps Guide
  1. In a pan, mix the Chicken, onion, mayo salt and peper
  2. in a big frying pan, melt the butter and bown the onion
  3. mix it with the chicken until its brown
  4. blend it with the ketchup and mustard
  5. pour the milk cream together, and take it out the fire, befor it boils
And thats it, it's done, enjoy!
